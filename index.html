How long does production take?

Our hand-finished frames typically take 7-10 business days. This includes digital surfacing of your custom lenses and a 12-point quality inspection.

Do you accept insurance?

While we do not bill insurance directly, we provide a detailed medical receipt suitable for out-of-network reimbursement from all major providers.

Can I book a virtual styling?

Yes. Our master opticians offer 20-minute video consultations to analyze face shape and recommend frames that suit your personal style and prescription.
